The Story

Hajime no Ippo is a Japanese boxing manga series written and illustrated by George Morikawa. The story follows Ippo Makunouchi, a high school student who begins his career in boxing and over time obtains many titles and defeats various opponents. Starting as a bullied teenager, Ippo transforms into a determined and skilled boxer, inspired by his experiences and the people he meets along his journey.

Characters

Ippo Makunouchi

The protagonist who starts his boxing career to become stronger and gain self-respect.

Mamoru Takamura

Ippo's mentor and an exceptional boxer known for his strength and charisma.

Ichirou Miyata

Ippo's rival, known for his speed and counter-punching abilities.
